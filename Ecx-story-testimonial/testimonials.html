From Students

Here are testimonies from students from ECX community.

Hassanat Busari

Front-End Web Development, University of Lagos

I went from knowing nothing about Frontend Web development to basically building websites by myself. It made me more open to learning also understand that, you can’t do everything by yourself. ECX made me believe that through learning, you can do anything. Even though I’ve had to learn some things by myself, I know it’s all for a good cause.

Moshood Mustapha

Back-End Web Development, University of Lagos

ECX has shaped me in my orientation and understanding of software development. Working with teams and reading other people's code also helped me and my career as a software developer. ECX is the best.

Simeon Taiwo

Front-End Web Development, Lagos/Unilag

ECX gave me the chance to know more about tech, which made me dive into Frontend development, giving me the opportunity to work with different developers from beginner to Senior level

Samuel Bamgbola

Python, University of Lagos

I started learning how to code officially this year and what are better way to start that with ECX. Got bored while learning Java and decided to attend the first Python class and got an insight. I pushed myself quite hard and participated in the 30daysofcode organised for April and May. I wouldn't have had a better way to get exposed to the world of coding without the superb guidance and reception from the wonderful tutors and the atmosphere at ECX.

Omolola Fele

Front-End Web Development, Ekiti State University

In my work as a success FrontEnd Developer and designer, I’ve had the opportunity to connect with people who’ve made a true and measurable impact in the world, including well-known experts(developers, Designers). But among this group of world influencers there are also everyday people who have found a special niche in which they’ve contributed at the highest level..thank you ECX

Iwinosa Igbinosa

Front-End Web Development, Unilag

When I joined ECX, I had almost 0% knowledge of Web development, ECX taught me the basics, shaped me, and brought out the best in me. I wouldn't have reached this height if not for ECX .

Ibukun Samuel

Front-End Web Development, University of Lagos

"ECX has made me better in so many ways.To start with, i never believed a business student like me could cope with programming, to the extent of being a front-end developer that has built so many website and applications,which i made them responsive to all screen.I've been able to apply for internships and got certified after completing all task given.I've been able to choose a framework which is ""React.js"" and i've started building applications alittle with it...All this testimonies wouldn't have happened if i had not join ECX. I want to use this medium to thank the organizers,the teachers and everyone that took their prescious time to make me grow each day as a developer.May God bless you all(Amen)"

Fortune Adekogbe

Python, Lagos

ECX was my first ever official Python teaching experience. It was a learning process and with help from the rest of the team, we made it work. Also with the 30 days of code challenge, as a mentor I was taught more about consistency, working under pressure and with deadlines. It was very interesting and I think those words don't describe it enough. Finally, mentoring machine learning interns in the ECX Internship 1.0 was a fulfilling experience. It was nice to see students go from being total beginners to developers who can work on interesting projects.

Idris Adeyemi Idris

Android/Mobile Development, University of Lagos

"A lot is all I can say. It helped me improve my python knowledge through hackathons and all. I started learning Flutter during the lockdown, with the ECX internship, I was able to add to my portfolio apps that I created within a short period of time. Also, I have been able to relate with different people through ECX, I'm normally an introvert but I have been able to manage it while working with people in teams at the internship and HackX. In summary, I have improved in both soft and hard skills, many thanks to ECX"

Nusaybah Fuad-Gafar

Front-End Web Development, Lagos/Unilag

I learned how to code thanks to ecx. I have also learnt a lot (from the next conference. Ecx helped me see myself in ways I never could have imagined

Angela Okafor

UI/UX, University of Lagos

ECX has been Impactful. It made me a better designer

Rukhayat Orekoya

Engineering Design, University of Lagos

ECX helped me kick start my journey as designer. At ECX I was able to actively contribute using my skills, build my confidence as designer, and connect with amazing minds. ECX helped me to believe in myself, in my skills and my capabilities

Ayomide Fagoroye

Python, University of lagos

I stopped programming because of a course and it's lecturer. But after participating in the 30 days of code, my love for programming grew. Now, I use flask, django, streamlit and data science with Python

I also got to learn several online programming resources and how to use them. It was a wonderful experience and i got to add several skills to my arsenal.
